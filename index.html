Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

How should I prep my hair?

Please arrive with your hair completely detangled, washed, and blow-dried straight with no products added unless you booked the wash and blow-dry add-on.

What is the late policy?

A 15-minute grace period is given. After 15 minutes, a $15 late fee will be applied. After 30 minutes, your appointment will be canceled and your deposit forfeited.

Is hair included in the price?

Hair is not included in the base price unless otherwise stated. Please check the specific style details or contact me beforehand.

Can I change my style the day of?

Style changes must be communicated at least 48 hours in advance to ensure enough time is allocated. Same-day changes are generally not permitted.
